FAQ

1What is #VegaFacts?

#VegaFacts is an interactive tool that aims to build an open knowledge repository. It's a part of the Vega Learning Management System, a.k.a. #VegaLMS

2 How to use this platform?

The first step for getting set up is to login with Twitter and fill out the #Vega form. There are a few things to keep in mind before submission:
- The QUOTE field can only contain up to 270 characters
- You need an active XRP address

1. Select the quote from your favorite article and paste it into the QUOTE field
2. Insert your XRP PUBLIC address in the corresponding field
3. Insert URL (the source of the quote)
4. Make sure you've filled out the form properly and click on Submit button

5 What are the rules?

- Don't spam
- Don't submit the same quote twice
- Don't share quotes from unknown or less reliable sources
- Don't cheat

3How does the payment get processed?

The default status of your submission is "pending". Submissions are approved manually and it may take some time. For each valid submission you get 0.05 XRP. Transactions happen on the mainnet and you don't need to take additional actions (no withdrwaval required)

4How is a valid submission defined?

Use high authority websites as a source of your submission. Each submission must be unique.

4 What about the daily submission limit?

Currently you can submit 10 quotes a day
